Green Lantern Corps

Overview

The Green Lantern Corps is an intergalactic law enforcement organization consisting of 7200 members who patrol the universe's 3600 sectors. Each Green Lantern wields a power ring, one of the most powerful weapons in the universe, fueled by willpower and the green light of the emotional spectrum.
